⚒ Shard Upgrades (Tier I to X)

Combine two shards of the exact same attribute and tier to get one shard of the next tier up (e.g., Tier I + Tier I ➔ Tier II).

To reach a maxed Tier X attribute, you need 512 Tier I shards in total! The number of shards needed to max out an attribute depends on its base rarity.

⚔ Applying to Gear (Syphoning)

Take an Attribute Shard to the Fusion Machine (Cathedral at Crimson Isle) or use /fusion to syphon its attribute onto any compatible armor/weapon.

If the gear already has that attribute, syphoning a higher-tier shard will instantly upgrade the gear's attribute level to the shard's level.

🛡 Gear Fusion Rules

You can also fuse two items of the same gear type (e.g., Crimson Helmet + Crimson Helmet) to combine their attributes:

  • If they have the same attribute, it upgrades by 1 level (up to max level 10).
  • If they have different attributes, the output gear will keep both attributes.
